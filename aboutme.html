Who is Game Jawns Inc?

Game Jawns Inc is me, Mike. I'm from Pennsylvania, working on this game in my spare time from my day job as a programmer. I like punk rock and video games.

I have been learning game dev in Godot and animation/drawing in order to make a game that combines my most nostalgic games (like ps1 RPGs) with my other favorites.

I gave myself lots of time to learn and focus on the same project. It's been fun to piece together and learn everything that's needed to make a game.

I've been working on JawnRPG for about 3 years, and I'm finally getting to a point where the systems I've worked on/thrown away/rewrote are starting to mesh with each other.

Planning on releasing this game on Steam and trying to get lots of people to enjoy it. If you've got any thoughts you can contact me at gamejawnsinc twitter/𝚐𝚖𝚊𝚒𝚕 or game_jawns_inc on reddit.
