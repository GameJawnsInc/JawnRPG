What is JawnRPG?

JawnRPG is my rpg project. Build a party with customizable jobs/skills/stats. Explore dungeons, dangerous paths, and towns. Throwback to ps1 rpgs mixed with flash games.

It's an ATB combat system (Chrono Trigger, FFIX, etc) with freedom of movement. Control your party manually, or with formations and AI. Party members will either cycle through off-cooldown skills, follow AI, or wait for manual control.
